Introduction

Welcome to Showtape Selection, a website dedicated to preserving the history of Chuck E Cheese, Showbiz Pizza Place, and more! We have a community of people who work every day, whether it would be in Showtape Central: our awesome showtape programming game, or writing and looking for unique material which has never been seen before. Whatever you want, it's right here and free to access.
